How to RSVP?

1. Log into your account on https://www.kemi.ai/.

2. On the home page, click on [Go]

You will see this interface :

3. ​Choose the meeting you want to attempt by clicking on [Select] and then [Yes].

You will see this interface :

4. Finally, click on the [Join Link] button and you will either see a waiting screen or directly join the meeting!

Once you complete the three first steps, you can then go to [My RSVP] section (on the top right of your screen). Here will be the list of meeting(s) you registered for and the link of the meeting(s) as shown here :
